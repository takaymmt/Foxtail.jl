## Indicators to ADD (not in current TODO list)

### Pivot Points ★★★★
- **Why**: Strongest self-fulfilling prophecy of any indicator. All traders see identical levels calculated from previous period OHLC. Embedded in many trading platform algorithms.
- **Types**: Standard, Fibonacci, Woodie, Camarilla, DM (Thomas DeMark)
- **Complexity**: Medium — requires "previous period" concept (daily/weekly/monthly pivot)
- **Priority**: HIGH

### Squeeze Momentum / TTM Squeeze ★★★★
- **Why**: 76,000+ likes on TradingView, one of the most popular community indicators ever created. Combines Bollinger Bands + Keltner Channel to detect volatility compression before breakout.
- **Requires**: Keltner Channel (TODO) + Bollinger Bands (already done)
- **Priority**: HIGH — implement after Keltner Channel

### Connors RSI ★★★
- **Why**: 3-component RSI composite (RSI + Streak RSI + %Rank) used heavily in systematic short-term mean-reversion strategies. 75%+ win rates in backtests vs simple RSI.
- **Used by**: Quantitative short-term traders, Larry Connors' methods
- **Complexity**: Medium — requires RSI (done) + streak calculation + percentile rank

### Anchored VWAP ★★★
- **Why**: VWAP anchored to a specific event (earnings, high/low) rather than session start. Growing institutional use. Retail adoption accelerating via TradingView.
- **Complexity**: Medium — variant of VWAP with custom start point

## Academic Research Findings (2023-2024)
- Trend-following (MAs, ADX) and mean-reversion (RSI, Bollinger Bands) have the most robust statistical evidence of profitability
- Combining multiple indicator types (momentum + volume + trend) consistently outperforms single-indicator strategies
- Individual indicator predictive value diminishes out-of-sample and is sensitive to transaction costs
- Volume indicators provide additive information beyond price-only indicators
- VWAP-based strategies show persistent alpha in intraday trading contexts
